Step by Step Guide

  1. Prep your nails
    • Use your emery boards to shape and buff your nails
    • Clean off the dust and any natural oils using acetone and cotton balls
  2. Put on your protective gloves!
    • We want our hands to stay beautiful and cancer free! Use the glove to block out potentially harmful rays
  3. Paint on a thin coat of Base Coat, covering the entire nail. Cure for 60-120 seconds (check your LED or UV light manual to see how long you need to cure)
  4. Paint on two to three thin layers of your base color, curing between each layer
    • Remember to cap the edge of your nail to make your manicure last longer
  5. Paint or stamp on any designs
    • This is where a dotting tool or stamp kit comes in handy
  6. Finish off with a Top Coat (usually you have to cure the top coat longer than the base coat and the gel polish, be sure to read the manual)
  7. Wipe away the tacky residual with acetone and cotton balls.
  8. Go show off your new nails, and make sure to brag about doing them yourself!
